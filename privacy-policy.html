Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

App name: StrideKai  ·  Website: stridekai.com  ·  Contact: support@stridekai.com

1. Overview

StrideKai ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the app, in accordance with applicable laws including the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and relevant United States privacy laws.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve the app.

Information you provide:

  • First name (for personalisation)
  • Notification preferences

App usage data:

  • Streak count
  • Walk history
  • Achievements and progress
  • App interactions (e.g. sessions, features used)

Automatically collected data:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • General usage data
  • Subscription status (via Apple)

3. Analytics and Tracking

We use third-party analytics and attribution tools to understand app usage and measure performance. These may include:

  • Mixpanel
  • AppsFlyer
  • TikTok (if enabled)

These services may collect device information, app usage events, approximate location (country or region level), and advertising identifiers where permitted. Some of this data may be used for attribution and advertising measurement purposes. We do not use this data to directly identify you.

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Where required by Apple, we will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. If you do not grant permission, tracking will be disabled and your app experience will not otherwise be affected.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use data to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Personalise your experience
  • Track activity and progress
  • Send notifications (if enabled)
  • Analyse usage and improve the app
  • Measure marketing and campaign performance

Legal basis (where applicable): Contract performance, Legitimate interests.

6. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers to operate the app, including analytics providers, attribution and marketing partners, and payment and subscription providers. These providers process data on our behalf and under their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data.

7. Data Storage

Core app data (e.g. name, progress) is stored locally on your device. Analytics and attribution data may be processed by third-party providers. We do not maintain our own centralised user database.

8. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until deleted. Analytics data is retained according to third-party provider policies.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by using the in-app delete option, or by deleting the app. This removes locally stored data from your device.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access your data, correct your data, request deletion, and restrict or object to processing. To exercise your rights, contact: support@stridekai.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g. the UK ICO).

11. Children's Privacy

StrideKai is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

12. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — payments and subscriptions
  • Adapty — subscription management
  • Mixpanel — analytics
  • AppsFlyer — attribution and analytics
  • TikTok — advertising attribution (if enabled)

Each provider processes data under their own privacy policies.

13. International Data Transfers

Some third-party providers may process data outside your country of residence. Where applicable, such transfers are carried out using appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

14. US Privacy Rights (California)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to know what personal data we collect, the right to request deletion, the right to correct inaccurate data, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data. We do not sell personal data. To exercise your rights, contact: support@stridekai.com.

15. Business Transfers

If StrideKai is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. In such cases, your data will remain protected under this Privacy Policy or an equivalent policy.

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
